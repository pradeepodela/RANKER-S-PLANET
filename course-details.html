Course Details

At Little Robo, we believe that every child should have the opportunity to learn about and explore the exciting world of robotics. That's why we offer a comprehensive program that teaches students how to build and program 7 different robots using the popular Arduino platform. Our hands-on approach, experienced instructors, and customizable curriculum make learning fun and engaging for kids of all ages. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced builder, Little Robo is the perfect place to start your robotics journey. With Little Robo, the future is in your hands. Join us now and discover your hidden talent!

Week 1: Introduction to Robotics and Arduino

* Introduction to robotics and its applications
* Overview of the Arduino microcontroller and its components
* Setting up the Arduino development environment

Week 2: Building a Line-Following Robot

* Understanding basic sensors and actuators
* Programming the Arduino to control the robot's movement
* Building and testing the line-following robot

Week 3: Building a Obstacle Avoiding Robot

* Understanding the use of ultrasonic sensors
* Programming the Arduino to detect and avoid obstacles
* Building and testing the obstacle-avoiding robot

Week 4: Building a Robotic Arm

* Understanding servo motors and their use in robotics
* Programming the Arduino to control the movement of the robotic arm
* Building and testing the robotic arm

Week 5: Building a Voice-Controlled Robot

* Understanding the use of voice recognition modules
* Programming the Arduino to respond to voice commands
* Building and testing the voice-controlled robot

Week 6: Building a Self-Balancing Robot

* Understanding the use of accelerometers and gyroscopes
* Programming the Arduino to maintain balance
* Building and testing the self-balancing robot

Week 7: Building a Sumo Robot

* Understanding the use of infrared sensors and motors
* Programming the Arduino for movement and collision detection
* Building and testing the sumo robot

Week 8: Final Project and Presentations

* Students will have the opportunity to work on a final project, utilizing the skills and knowledge gained throughout the course
* Students will
* Students will present their final projects to their peers and parents
* Students will receive a certificate of completion


NOTE: The curriculum can be adjusted and modified based on the students' learning level and interests.
